Argentina

Argentina is a large South American country with terrain that includes the Andes mountains, glacial lakes and grasslands in the Pampas, the traditional grazing land of its famous cattle. The country is known for tango dance and music. Its great cosmopolitan capital, Buenos Aires, is centered on the Plaza de Mayo, surrounded by imposing 19th-century buildings, such as the Casa Rosada, the iconic presidential palace.
